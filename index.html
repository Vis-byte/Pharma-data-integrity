The Problem in Pharma Labs

Many pharmaceutical laboratories struggle to prove that analytical and batch data from systems such as HPLC, GC, LIMS, and ELN has not been modified. Missing audit trails, manual processes, and fragmented systems create stress during inspections and audits.

What is ALCOA and Why It Matters

ALCOA is a set of data integrity principles followed in regulated pharmaceutical environments to ensure that data is reliable, traceable, and trustworthy throughout its lifecycle.

Regulatory authorities expect laboratories to demonstrate ALCOA compliance. In real lab environments, this is often difficult due to limited visibility into data changes and weak audit evidence.

Attributable

Every action and data entry is linked to a specific user and system.

Legible

Data remains readable, structured, and understandable.

Contemporaneous

Data is recorded at the time the activity occurs.

Original

Original source data is preserved without overwriting.

Accurate

Data accuracy is protected using integrity controls.

Complete & Consistent

Full data history is maintained without unexplained gaps.

Our Approach

This platform focuses on monitoring data integrity rather than simply storing data. It provides traceability, tamper detection, and clear audit evidence to help laboratories demonstrate trust in their data.

This is an early-stage platform designed to support compliance efforts. It does not replace regulatory responsibilities or validation activities.
